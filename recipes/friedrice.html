Fried Rice Recipe

Description

A recipe that will help you make savory and scrumptious fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Veggies
  • Meat
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Cook eggs
  2. Cook meat
  3. Add veggies
  4. Stir-fry rice separately
  5. Add together
  6. Season
