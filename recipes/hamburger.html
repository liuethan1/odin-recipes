Hamburger Recipe

Description

This amazing recipe will help you create a mouthwatering and savory burger.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Egg
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
